Studio Ghibli Inc. (株式会社スタジオジブリ) is a Japanese animation studio. It is best known for its animated feature films. Its mascot and most recognizable symbol is a character named Totoro. These films have drastically transformed the world of animated films with their superior filmmaking techniques and profound scriptwriting. Among the studio's highest-grossing films are Spirited Away (2001), Howl's Moving Castle (2004) and Ponyo (2008). Some of these films have received nominations and have won some of them.
